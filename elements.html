(i) Smart Shoes for Blind People


This is “Smart Shoe”. It is mainly used by Blind people”. Let’s see its uses – When any obstacle/object comes near this shoe, the buzzer of that shoe will beep. Through this way my shoe is very useful for Blind people.


Working

• Our project will programmable for certain distance measurement and sense the object from that particular distance and get the buzzer on indicate to the which is use our product to that some object or distraction will be in their way.
• It will very useful to indicate proper direction for the blind people.

Materials that are use:-



(i) Arduino(UNO):-


It is used for programming.


(ii) Ultrasonic Sensor:-


It is used to sense any object /obstacles.


(iii) Buzzer:-


It is used for beeping
